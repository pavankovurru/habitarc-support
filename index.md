  ## Getting Started

  **What habit types are available?**
  - Positive: build good habits (exercise, reading).
  - Negative: break habits you’re avoiding.
  - Timed: track minutes spent (e.g., 30 minutes meditation).
  - Quantity: log amounts (e.g., 8 glasses of water).

  **How do I track completion?**
  Tap the checkmark beside a habit on the Today screen. Timed habits prompt for duration;
  quantity habits prompt for an amount. Streaks and progress update instantly.

  ---

  ## Habit Tracking

  **What scheduling options can I use?**
  - Daily: every day.
  - Weekly: specific weekdays.
  - Custom: every _N_ days or weeks.

  **How are streaks calculated?**
  Streaks count consecutive scheduled days completed. Missing a scheduled day resets the streak;
  completing the habit starts a new streak.

  **Can I edit or delete habits?**
  Long-press any habit to edit, archive, or delete it. Editing keeps your history; deleting
  removes it permanently.

  ## Data & Sync

  **Where is my data stored?**
  Habitarc stores your habits in your personal iCloud account using Apple’s CloudKit, with local
  storage as backup for offline access.

  **Does Habitarc sync across devices?**
  Yes. iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch stay in sync automatically through iCloud when you’re signed
  in with the same Apple ID.

  **Can I use Habitarc without iCloud?**
  The app keeps working offline with local storage and syncs when iCloud becomes available again.

  **Can I export my data?**
  Exporting isn’t available yet. Your data remains safely in your iCloud account.

  ## Notifications & Reminders

  **How do I set reminders?**
  When creating or editing a habit, toggle “Reminder” and choose a time. Accept the notification
  permission prompt if you haven’t already.

  **Why am I not receiving notifications?**
  Check:
  1. iOS Settings → Notifications → Habitarc → Allow Notifications.
  2. Focus / Do Not Disturb isn’t blocking alerts.
  3. Reminder time is in the future.
  4. Habit is active and scheduled for today.
  5. Relaunch the app if needed.

  **Can I complete habits from a notification?**
  Yes. Use the quick actions to mark complete or snooze without opening the app.

  ## Troubleshooting

  **Habits aren’t syncing between devices**
  - Confirm both devices use the same Apple ID.
  - Ensure iCloud Drive and Habitarc sync are enabled.
  - Force close and reopen Habitarc.
  - Check your internet connection.
  - Wait a few minutes and retry.
  - Restart both devices if the issue persists.

  **The app feels slow or unresponsive**
  - Force close and reopen Habitarc.
  - Restart your device.
  - Free up storage space.
  - Update to the latest Habitarc and iOS versions.
